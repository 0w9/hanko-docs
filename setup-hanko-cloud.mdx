### You need to just follow these 5 quick steps.

<Steps>
<Step title="Sign Up">
    To begin, head over to [Hanko](https://cloud.hanko.io/) and sign up. 
    <Frame>
    <img width="500" src="/images/get-started/create-acc.png" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Create an Organization">
    Once you've signed up, you'll be directed to create an organization.
    <Frame>
    <img width="500" src="/images/get-started/create-org.png" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Initiate a New Project">
    With your organization set, it's time to kick off a new project. Choose 'Authentication and user management' and click on 'Create project'.
    <Frame>
    <img src="/images/get-started/create-proj.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}/>
    </Frame>

    Input your project name and provide your app's URL. For the app URL, make sure to enter the full URL including `http://` or `https://` and the port in case it's localhost, e.g. `http://localhost:3000`.
    <Frame>
    <img src="/images/get-started/create-new-project-modal.png" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}/>
    </Frame>

</Step>
<Step title="Copy the API URL">
    Upon entering the Hanko console, copy the API URL. You'll need this for future integrations.
    <Frame>
    <img width="500" src="/images/get-started/api-url.png" />
    </Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Dive into Integrations">
    You're all set! Visit the [quickstarts](/quickstarts/fullstack/next) section to kick off your integration journey with your favorite frameworks.
</Step>
</Steps>
